# 🔍 The Problem

> ⚠️ **What triggered this review:**
> - Ad spend jumped from ~$71/wk to ~$398/wk (+464%) but sales only grew +14%
> - ACOS exploded from 27.9% (March) to 122.1% (April)
> - CPC nearly doubled: $1.89 → $3.04
> - CVR dropped by half: 20.1% → 11.5%
> - TACoS went from a healthy 4.4% to a dangerous 21.5%
> - **Every extra $1 spent on ads was returning only $0.51 in revenue**

---

# 🔎 What We Discovered

> 📊 **The Root Cause:**
> Country Pet **dominates** on pet-specific cod liver oil terms but **gets destroyed** on generic/broad terms.

**Where we win:**
- ✅ "pet cod liver oil" → **8.9% ACOS** (100% CVR)
- ✅ "cod liver oil dog" → **10.9% ACOS** (75% CVR)
- ✅ "cod liver oil liquid for dogs" → **12.7% ACOS** (67% CVR)
- ✅ ASIN targets → **<2% ACOS**

**Where we lose:**
- ❌ "cod liver oil" (generic) → **154% ACOS** -- too broad, human buyers
- ❌ All fish oil terms → **89% ACOS** historically, **0 sales** in target week
- ❌ All omega 3 terms → **$28 spent, ZERO sales ever**
- ❌ Competitor brands (nordic naturals etc.) → **$47 wasted**
- ❌ Wrong category (raw dog food, lamb, organs etc.) → **$67 wasted**

> 🔴 **Total wasted spend: ~$155+ (19% of total ad budget)**

# 🛠️ What We Did

## 1. Campaign Restructure

**Old:** 7 mixed campaigns, generic terms eating budget, no keyword isolation

**New (6 focused campaigns):**

| # | Campaign | Type | Keywords | Default Bid |
|---|---------|------|----------|:----------:|
| 1 | **CLO - Auto** | Auto | Refined -- pet-specific only, generics removed | **$1.20** |
| 2 | **CLO - Category Targeting** | Auto | Category targeting | **$1.20** |
| 3 | **CLO - Hero Keywords** | Manual | "cod liver oil for dogs", "cod liver oil for cats" | **$1.20** |
| 4 | **CLO - Fish Oil** | Manual | "fish oil for dogs", "fish oil for cats" | **$1.20** |
| 5 | **CLO - Omega** | Manual | "omega 3 for dogs", "omega 3 for cats" | **$1.20** |
| 6 | **Dog Treats - Auto** | Auto | Auto targeting | **$1.20** |

> 💡 **Why $1.20 bid?** Baseline avg CPC was ~$2.00-2.50. At 60% = ~$1.20-1.50. With Up & Down bidding (can increase by 100%), Amazon can push to ~$2.40 for high-converting placements. This keeps average CPC near baseline instead of the inflated $3-5.

> 💡 **Why separate Fish Oil & Omega campaigns?** These never converted when mixed with CLO. Isolating them lets us test if pet-specific terms ("fish oil **for dogs**") convert better than generic terms. If they don't, we cut them without affecting CLO performance.

# 📈 Why This Strategy

## TACoS & Breakeven Math

- Current TACoS: ~38%
- Ad Sales %: 28% → breakeven ROAS = 1.08
- Q1 repeat rate: 25% → adjusting for LTV, breakeven ROAS = 0.77
- **Target: keep ROAS above 1.3.** Currently at 1.12 -- close but CPC needs to come down
- Revenue tracking ahead of March in half of April -- **we are growing aggressively**

## The Core Insight

> 💡 Brand converts on **"cod liver oil for dogs/cats"** but the moment you go broad ("cod liver oil") or into adjacent categories (fish oil, omega 3) **without pet-specific qualifiers**, conversions drop to zero.
>
> **Strategy:** Own the pet-specific niche. Carefully test adjacent categories in isolated campaigns. Don't let generic terms eat the budget.
